# Privacy Policy — Hover Image Preview

Effective date: February 21, 2026

Hover Image Preview respects your privacy. This policy explains what data the extension uses and why.

## Summary
- We do not sell personal data.
- We do not run ads.
- We do not collect account passwords, payment data, or personal profile details.

## What the extension does
Hover Image Preview provides:
1. Image preview on hover for links.
2. On-demand word lookup (definition, synonyms, antonyms, and source links) from selected text.

## Data the extension accesses

### 1) Website link URLs
- Purpose: determine whether a hovered link points to an image and display preview.
- Where processed: in your browser while you use the extension.

### 2) Selected word text (when you request a definition)
- Purpose: fetch definition and related language data.
- Where sent: free dictionary/reference services used by the extension.

### 3) Local/synced extension settings and lookup history
- Purpose: save your preferences and recent lookups.
- Storage: Chrome storage (including sync storage if enabled on your Google account).

## Third-party services
When you request a word definition, the selected word may be requested from:
- Dictionary API (dictionaryapi.dev)
- Datamuse API (api.datamuse.com)
- Wikipedia REST API (wikipedia.org)
- Merriam-Webster website links may be shown for reference

When this occurs, those services may receive technical metadata (for example IP address and request headers) as part of normal web requests.

## Data sharing and selling
- We do not sell your data.
- We do not share personal data for advertising.
- We only send the minimum request data needed to return dictionary/reference results.

## Data retention
- Word history is stored in Chrome extension storage and can be cleared by the user.
- Sync behavior depends on your Chrome account sync settings.

## Your choices
- You can disable or remove the extension at any time.
- You can clear stored word history in the extension options.
- You can disable Chrome Sync if you do not want cross-device syncing.

## Security
We use Chrome extension storage and standard browser network requests. No method of transmission or storage is guaranteed 100% secure.

## Children
This extension is not directed to children under 13.

## Changes to this policy
We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted with a revised effective date.
